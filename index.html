Who We Are

We are Civil Society, a team of experienced educational specialists that provide services such as high-impact tutoring, counseling, consulting, and mentorship. We actively assist young minds reach their full academic potential and become more confident in their scholastic pursuits. We’ve found rewarding success in helping our students understand the value of their voices and giving them a chance to be heard without judgment. This creates an environment of mutual trust and openness. Allowing us to effectively guide our students with positive reinforcement and healthy connections! We are excited to connect and discuss the future success of the new generation. If you have questions or would like further information, feel free to reach out to us.

Our Approach

Our approach is unique and comprehensive, focusing on the development of foundational skills to ensure students are better prepared to navigate challenges that come with social/cultural pressures and demanding academic achievement. In our endeavors, we initiate projects by conducting a comprehensive assessment and evaluation of existing circumstances. This meticulous process allows us to customize our efforts to fit the unique context within the district, ensuring that local realities are seamlessly integrated into the framework of evidence-based and research-based practices. Civil Society’s assessments of school performance are consistently grounded in the evidence we gather through our systematic approach. Our mission is to revolutionize learning by creating programs that support our existing academic institutions.
